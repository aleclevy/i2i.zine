Call for Submissions

Follow our social media to be the first updated on new calls for submissions!

We Accept ALL Forms of Art!

Submission ideas include, but are not limited to:

  • Photography
  • Short stories
  • Poems
  • Prose
  • Personal & instructional essays
  • Persuasive essays
  • Interviews
  • Visual & multimedia art
  • Paintings & collages
  • Graphic design
  • Song lyrics & music
  • And more!

How to Submit

  1. Know the theme: Check our site, submission opening posts, or Instagram highlights for theme information and inspiration.
  2. Prepare your work: Submit old work that fits the theme, or create something new!
  3. Sell yourself and your piece: Include your name, age, a 2-3 sentence bio (optional), and a 2-3 sentence explanation of your piece (required).
  4. Submit for consideration: Send your name, information, piece, and art description to i2izinelb@gmail.com.
